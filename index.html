SuperStore Global Sales Performance Dashboard

Interactive Excel dashboard analysing 51,290 orders across 7 global markets from 2011 to 2014. Built using pivot tables, SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, slicers, and four different chart types. The dashboard answers key business questions on profitability, discount impact, regional performance, and sub-category losses.

Key analytical findings
  • Technology generates the highest revenue at $4,744,691 from only 51,290 orders — highest revenue per order of all categories
  • High Discount orders average -$71.92 profit versus $57.15 for Low Discount orders — discounting is the primary profit destroyer
  • Tables is the biggest loss-making sub-category losing $64,083 across the dataset — urgent pricing review needed
  • Annual sales grew 90% from $2,259,511 in 2011 to $4,300,841 in 2014 — consistent year-on-year growth
  • West region is the top performing region with highest total sales across all segments

Road Accident Casualty Dashboard

Interactive Excel dashboard analysing road accident casualty data across 2021 and 2022. Built using pivot tables, slicers, donut charts, bar charts, and a line trend chart. Filters by year, accident date, and urban/rural area allow dynamic exploration of 417,883 total casualties.

Key analytical findings
  • Total casualties: 417,883 across the analysis period
  • Fatal casualties represent only 1.7% of total — slight injuries dominate at 83.9%
  • Single carriageway roads account for 309,698 casualties — highest by road type
  • Dry road surfaces produce the most casualties (384,769) — counter-intuitive finding
  • Urban areas record significantly higher casualties than rural areas across both years
